How Types Connect

Relationship Compatibility

Cognitive functions shape how types relate to each other. Some pairs flow easily — others create growth through friction.

NF — The Idealists

NF types connect through shared values and meaning. Friendships form fast and run deep — sometimes too intense for other temperaments.

INFP INFJ ENFP ENFJ

Pair well with: NF + NT for intellectual depth · NF + NF for emotional resonance

NT — The Analysts

NT friendships are built on ideas and mutual respect for competence. They love a good debate and don't need constant contact to maintain closeness.

INTP INTJ ENTP ENTJ

Pair well with: NT + NF for inspiration · NT + NT for intellectual sparring

SJ — The Guardians

SJ friendships are steady and dependable. They show care through action — remembering details, showing up, following through. Loyalty is everything.

ISFJ ISTJ ESFJ ESTJ

Pair well with: SJ + SP for shared groundedness · SJ + NF for balance of warmth

SP — The Explorers

SP friendships are energetic and present. They bond through doing things together — adventure, craft, experience. They don't overthink relationships.

ISFP ISTP ESFP ESTP

Pair well with: SP + SJ for stability and fun · SP + NT for challenge and play
